About StarBoard

Written by Tobias Waters


StarBoard:

StarBoard is a dashboard for stargazers. The homepage includes a visualization of the moon and its phase,
and weather for the next 12 hours. Included in the weather is GazingScore, a number from 0-100 that
tells how good the quality of stargazing is at that time. 100 is perfect, 70 is good, 40 is passable,
and 0 is impossible. Make sure to turn on location here to allow for weather info and StarFinder capabilities!

StarFinder:

StarFinder shows the 10 brightest objects in the sky. On a clear, dark night, you should be able to see all 10!
They are ranked by apparent magnitude (brightness from Earth), where lower magnitude means a brighter object.
Below, you can see a map of the sky, centered facing completely vertical towards north at your location. to move,
use your mouse to pan. Or, you can click on one of the 10 bright objects and press the "Move To" button below
the sky map. Happy stargazing!
